=======================================================================================================
IMPORTANT: WireUpCoreRuntime and other errors; Need to switch from x86 to x64 OR vice versa to fix this
=======================================================================================================

This has been happening to me on the lab machines on campus where I would get various build fails. 
One of these fails mentions something about a **WireUpCoreRuntime**. The fix is to switch from x86 to x64 OR vice versa.
